Texas Instruments Innovation Challenge: Design Contest 2015 News

The CTSN team has entered the TI Design Contest, and has received $200 from TI for purchase of evaluation modules, and free t-shirts. They have a chance to win $15,000 in first place prize money. Any further news about their progress in the competition will be posted here.
